Fried Rice

Description

Hereâ€™s a step-by-step recipe for making delicious fried rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1/2 cup diced meat (chicken, pork, or shrimp)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Dice the meat and chop the vegetables
  2. Cook Eggs: Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan or wok.
    Add the beaten eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Cook Meat and vegetables: In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed.
    Cook the garlic and onion until fragrant. Add the diced meat and cook until browned.
    Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Combine and Season: Add the cold rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps.
    Stir everything together and add the soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Finish: Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan and stir to combine.
    Cook for another minute or two until everything is heated through.
