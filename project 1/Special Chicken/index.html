Special Chicken

Description

Here’s a step-by-step recipe for making delicious fried rice

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven:!Preheat Oven Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season Chicken:!Season Chicken Rub the chicken breasts with olive oil,
    salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Sear Chicken:!Sear Chicken Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
    Add the chicken and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  4. Bake Chicken:!Bake Chicken Transfer the seared chicken to a baking dish.
    Add minced garlic on top. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes until fully cooked.
  5. Garnish and Serve:!Garnish and Serve Sprinkle chopped parsley over the chicken before serving.
    Enjoy your delicious chicken dish!
